Nico Bakomihalis¶

Nico Bakomihalis is a software engineer with a passion for writing and reading wonderful Python code. Nico specializes in creating packages that have an intuitive API, and are fun to use. When not needlessly refactoring otherwise decent code, Nico enjoys going for hikes, rock climbing, and spending time with friends and family.
