Welcome

My name is Nico. I am a software engineer with a firm belief that writing code is both a scientific and artistic process.

My goal is to create software that improves the lives of all people who interact with it. Depending on the user, this can mean different things.

For a customer, this means that the software is easy to use and does what it is supposed to do. For a developer, this means that the software is easy to understand and maintain.

This site is just a simple way to claim a tiny piece of the internet, and to build simple tools. I hope you enjoy it.
